Canggu
4 days · first stop
Where I stayed

Villa Solaya

Big Highlight

Brand new, incredible design — one of those places that genuinely stops you. Super aesthetic, beautifully finished, very conveniently located close to everything. One note: our unit didn't get direct sun on the pool, worth checking when you book. Found it on Booking.com at a better price than Airbnb.

Book on Booking.com — better price than Airbnb
Breakfast & cafes

Brunch Club Pererenan

Must Visit

Huge open rooftop, tables, open space — and a massive tree right in the middle of the café. The kind of place that actually has soul. The atmosphere is perfect. Order the pancake. Don't skip it.

The pancake is non-negotiable

Zai Cafe

Personal Fav

One of my top spots in Canggu. The fruit plate bowl and eggs are excellent. Everything tastes fresh and consistent. The kind of place you return to without hesitation.

Nude Cafe

Cafe

Small and cozy. Great for a slower morning when you want something more intimate.

Ele Restaurant

Breakfast + Dinner

Works well for both breakfast and a healthy dinner. A reliable spot for clean eating.

Fitness

"The fitness lifestyle in Bali is unlike anything I've seen. Hyrox, reformer, boxing, pilates — everywhere. The people here genuinely take care of their bodies."

#1 in Canggu

Reload Sanctuary

Top Pick

Brand new. Chic crowd, beautiful design, restaurant on site, full recovery area. Simply a place that can only exist in Bali. You won't find this anywhere else.

#2 in Canggu

Obsidian

Gym

Serious facility. The gym itself is beautiful — great pool, sauna, restaurant. Crowd leans influencer and competitive bodybuilder — not my vibe personally, but objectively a stunning gym.

#3 in Canggu

OMNI

Gym

Very aesthetic. Small training floor, open rooftop area, decent pool. One thing I didn't like: you can't use your laptop in the restaurant — they charge extra for the coworking space. Small thing but it bothered me.

Dining, shopping & sunset

Ina Ré Bali

Fine Dining

Desa Kitsune — same group as Savaya. Mediterranean-leaning, standout paella, refined atmosphere. Yes, it's expensive by Bali standards. But this is fine dining. Beautiful pool, café for breakfast, big DJ nights regularly. A full-day spot.

Open House

Seseh

Great spot in Pererenan — healthy food where you build your own plate, sauna, pool, and nice house music running in the background. The kind of place that somehow does everything well without feeling like it's trying. A full experience, not just a meal.

La Brisa

Sunset

Best sunset spot in Canggu. Not the fanciest — but the vibe is exactly right. Table fully redeemable. Food is good, cocktails work.

Book a table facing the water

Karja Concept Store

Shopping

My favourite clothing shop. Wide linen collection — shirts, trousers, men and women. I did a shoot for them (partners) but the recommendation stands on its own.

Wellness & experiences

Pressure Spa

Must Experience

An amazing massage experience — but what makes it stand out is the space itself. Highly aesthetic, deeply atmospheric, fully Instagrammable. One of those places you remember not just for the treatment but for how it felt to be there. Don't miss it.

Hotels worth visiting

Nirjhara Bali

Stay

Canopy suite — a duplex with a massive rooftop bathtub, surrounded by trees and greenery, with a waterfall nearby. Service is a 10/10. Go here to disconnect.

Ulaman Eco Resort

Day Pass

The bamboo resort that went viral — and earned it. Spa, yoga, beautiful aesthetic. Day passes available. Worth every minute.

Ubud
Day trip · rice fields & jungle
Where I stayed

Prana Ubud

Big Highlight

Villa built like it's inside a container — in the best way. Breathtaking views, infinity pool, incredible design, great service. Yes, it's a bit far from the Ubud centre. Worth every minute of that drive.

Must do

Tegalalang Rice Fields

Non-Negotiable

Go to Bali and skip the rice fields? Not an option. The terraces are extraordinary — the photos you'll take here are unlike anything else. A genuine once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Go early morning to beat the crowd

Sacred Monkey Forest

Activity

Classic Ubud stop. Entertaining, fun, a bit chaotic. Keep food and sunglasses away from the monkeys — they will take them.

Uluwatu
Final days · cliffs & ocean
Where I stayed

Villa Larente

Bingin Beach

The interior design is exceptional — modern, curved glass wall, two separate bedrooms, great pool. Found on Booking.com. Bingin Beach as a location was okay but may not suit everyone. The villa itself was absolutely worth it.

Fitness

Bambu Fitness

The Iconic One

The most famous gym in Uluwatu — and the most different from Canggu. The CrossFit area is enormous. Machine area is smaller than expected. The crowd is shirtless, surf-culture, outdoor energy. Not cheap. Pool area is a 10/10.

Breakfast & cafes

Artisan Cafe

Fav

Build-your-own breakfast. Great vibe, flexible menu, enjoyable from the moment you sit down.

Chela Bali

Breakfast

Super aesthetic space. Food 8/10. Service was decent but I expected a bit more given the setting.

Dining

Teja Restaurant

Best of the Trip

The best restaurant of my entire trip. Fine dining — sophisticated location, beautiful setting, flawless and fast service, food that actually lives up to all of it. A genuine 10/10. If you do one fine dining in Uluwatu, it's this one.

Kala Restaurant

Greek · 7/10

Greek concept. Decent food — solid 7/10. Good when you want a break from Asian flavours. Not a must, but a good option to have.
